I've Been Exactly Where You Are

I'm Susana. Mom of three kids. At 46, perimenopause hit me like a truck - literally overnight.

Before I turned 40, I had successfully lost over 40kg with keto after my pregnancies. It worked beautifully. Until it didn't.

When perimenopause started, the same keto approach that helped me before just... stopped working. The sleep issues started. The hot flashes. The exhaustion. I felt like I was living in someone else's body.

Then something changed: Four years ago, I moved to Spain. And I was surrounded by what they call "the world's healthiest diet" - real Mediterranean food. Olive oil. Fresh fish. Vegetables from the market. Simple, whole ingredients.

I started combining my keto knowledge with these Mediterranean foods. Not processed "keto products" from packages - just real food the way Spanish grandmothers have been eating for generations.

The sleep issues got better. The hot flashes eased. I started feeling like myself again.

As a web developer, I thought: if this worked for me, maybe it can help other women going through the same thing. So I built this website.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free trial?

The free trial gives you 3 days of meal ideas with names, descriptions, and prep times - enough to know if this approach works for you. Shopping lists, full recipes with instructions, and ongoing weekly plans require a paid subscription ($8.99/month).

Do I need to follow these plans exactly?

Not at all. These are inspiration, not strict rules. Swap ingredients, adjust portions, skip meals that don't fit your schedule. The goal is to make life easier, not harder.

Why not just use American keto products?

Many women find that processed keto foods (fake cheese, protein bars, artificial sweeteners) don't make them feel good. Real Mediterranean foods - the kind your grandmother would recognize - are simpler and often feel better. This isn't medical advice, just what thousands of women have shared with us.

I'm not great at cooking. Will this work for me?

Absolutely. These are simple, whole-food recipes (15-30 minutes) with everyday ingredients. No chef skills required. If you can chop vegetables and turn on a stove, you're good to go.

What if I don't like certain foods?

Tell us in the form! We'll suggest alternatives based on your preferences. Don't like salmon? We'll give you chicken or lamb options. Hate Brussels sprouts? We'll swap them for broccoli.

How is this different from other meal plans?

Most plans rely on processed "keto" products or are generic. Ours focus on real Mediterranean foods that women 40+ have told us make them feel better. No plastic cheese. No protein powder. Just simple food ideas.

Is this medical or nutritional advice?

No. This is a meal planning inspiration service, not medical or nutritional counseling. We share ideas based on what has worked for other women and Mediterranean eating principles. Always consult your healthcare provider before making significant dietary changes, especially if you have health conditions or take medications.
