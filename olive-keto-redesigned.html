Why Real Food Matters After 40

Not all "keto" is created equal. Your changing body needs nutrients, not chemicals.

❌ American "Keto" Products

  • Processed cheese products - inflammatory oils, artificial flavors
  • Protein bars & shakes - sweeteners that disrupt hormones
  • Keto bread & pasta - wheat protein isolates, gums, fillers
  • Fat bombs - pure calories, no nutrition
  • MCT oil obsession - isolated fats without cofactors

Result: Weight loss plateau, worsening perimenopause symptoms, digestive issues

✓ Mediterranean Whole Foods

  • Real cheese from sheep/goat - easier digestion, complete nutrition
  • Wild-caught fish - omega-3s for brain fog & hot flashes
  • Organic vegetables - fiber for hormone elimination
  • Extra virgin olive oil - polyphenols reduce inflammation
  • Nuts, seeds, legumes - phytoestrogens ease symptoms

Result: Sustained energy, clearer thinking, easier perimenopause, lasting weight management

Less processing = better wellbeing. Your grandmother knew this. Science confirms it.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free week?

The free week gives you 7 days of meal names and prep times - enough to know if this approach works for you. Shopping lists, full recipes with instructions, and ongoing weekly plans require a paid subscription ($8.99/month).

Do I need to follow these plans exactly?

Not at all. These are inspiration, not strict rules. Swap ingredients, adjust portions, skip meals that don't fit your schedule. The goal is to make life easier, not harder.

Why not just use American keto products?

Processed keto foods (fake cheese, protein bars, artificial sweeteners) often worsen perimenopause symptoms like hot flashes and brain fog. Real Mediterranean foods provide the complete nutrition your changing body needs, not just macros.

I'm not great at cooking. Will this work for me?

Absolutely. These are simple, whole-food recipes (15-30 minutes) with everyday ingredients. No chef skills required. If you can chop vegetables and turn on a stove, you're good to go.

What if I don't like certain foods?
